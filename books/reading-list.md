# Reading List

The book club reading itinerary, focused on product thinking and leadership.

## Book Club Itinerary

### 1. Escaping the Build Trap - Melissa Perri
*How Effective Product Management Creates Real Value*

Why: Foundation for understanding product thinking vs. feature factories. Helps teams move from output-focused to outcome-focused work.

### 2. The Art of Action - Stephen Bungay
*How Leaders Close the Gaps Between Plans, Actions, and Results*

Why: Strategic alignment and empowering teams to act with intent. Bridging strategy and execution.

### 3. Amp It Up - Frank Slootman
*Leading for Hypergrowth by Raising Expectations, Increasing Urgency, and Elevating Intensity*

Why: Leadership and execution. How to raise the bar and drive results.

### 4. Inspired - Marty Cagan
*How to Create Tech Products Customers Love*

Why: How the best product teams work. Covers discovery, delivery, and product culture. Recommended by a colleague.
