# Book Club Positioning

How to describe and market the book club.

## Elevator Pitch (Draft)
A monthly book club for IT professionals who want to think beyond code. We read books about product management, leadership, and strategy to raise our awareness above day-to-day tasks and understand the bigger picture of building products.

## Key Messages

**For Virtual:**
- Targeted at IT professionals who want to grow
- Product-focused reading to complement technical skills
- Monthly meetings to discuss what we've learned
- Low commitment: one book per month, one hour discussion

**For Physical (Local):**
- Local community of tech professionals
- Same great reading list, in-person discussion
- Networking with others thinking about product and strategy

## Value Proposition
- Learn how product managers think
- Understand business and product strategy
- Discuss ideas with peers facing similar challenges
- Grow beyond individual contributor mindset

## Tone
- Approachable, not academic
- Practical, not theoretical
- For practitioners, by practitioners
- "I've been reading these books and they've helped me - want to join?"

## Origin Story (for credibility)
Started reading product books to raise awareness beyond code. Found it helpful. Discovered others had similar interests. Reviving/creating a book club to discuss together.
